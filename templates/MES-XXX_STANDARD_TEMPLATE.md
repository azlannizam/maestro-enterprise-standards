## 1. Purpose

solves. Describe the outcome the enterprise expects when the standard is
followed.

## 2. Scope

Name the projects, activities, or artifacts in scope. Product-specific detail
belongs in product repositories, not here.

## 3. Principles

List the durable principles that underpin this standard. Principles explain the
"why" and rarely change between versions.

## 4. Mandatory Requirements

"REQUIRED" per RFC 2119. Each requirement should be testable and unambiguous.

## 5. Recommended Practices

"MAY". These represent proven good practice without blocking compliance.

## 6. Exceptions

recorded. Every exception must be documented, time-bound, and owned.
